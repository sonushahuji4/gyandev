## Introduction

GyanDev is a content-first technical education site operated by Sonu Shahuji
from India. This policy explains what data the site interacts with, what it
deliberately avoids, and the rights you have.

Privacy questions: [privacy@gyandev.org](mailto:privacy@gyandev.org).

## What We Collect

- **Nothing by default.** No accounts, no sign-up forms, no cookies used to
  identify you.
- **`localStorage` in your browser.** Reading progress, theme preference, and
  bookmarks live only on your device. Clearing your site data erases them.
- **Cloudflare Web Analytics.** Anonymous, cookie-less pageview counts. IPs are
  truncated on arrival; no visitor profile is built.
- **Giscus comments.** If you choose to comment, your GitHub profile is shown
  publicly under the comment — per GitHub's policy, not ours.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email address) unless you contact us
  directly.
- No tracking cookies.
- No third-party trackers — no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no
  advertising networks.
- No browser fingerprinting.
- No sale of your data. The site has nothing to sell.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

- **Essential preferences** (theme, active tab): stored in `localStorage`, not
  cookies.
- **Analytics**: Cloudflare Web Analytics is cookie-less.
- **Third-party**: Giscus sets GitHub cookies only if you interact with the
  comments widget; GitHub's cookie policy applies in that case.
- **No marketing cookies.**
- **No consent banner needed** because the site sets no cookies that require
  consent under GDPR, ePrivacy, or CCPA.

## How Your Data Is Used

- Data in `localStorage` stays on your device. It is never transmitted to our
  servers.
- Cloudflare Analytics returns aggregate stats (pageviews, referrers,
  countries) used to improve the content catalog.
- Giscus comments are published publicly on the associated GitHub Discussion
  at the moment you post them.

## Data Sharing

- We do not share your data because we do not hold any that is not already
  public.
- Giscus comments live on GitHub Discussions, which is public by design.
- Cloudflare is a data processor handling delivery and analytics on our
  behalf; their Data Processing Agreement governs that relationship.

## Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you have rights under GDPR (EU / EEA), UK-DPA
(United Kingdom), CCPA/CPRA (California), and DPDPA 2023 (India). Because the
site holds no identifiable account data, most rights are effectively
self-serve:

- **Access.** We hold no stored personal data about you.
- **Erasure.** Clear your browser site data at any time to remove progress,
  preferences, and bookmarks.
- **Objection to processing.** Disable `localStorage` in your browser or use
  a private window.
- **Data portability** (Phase 2). A progress-export button is planned on
  `/settings`; until it ships, `localStorage` keys under `gyandev:v1:*` can
  be exported manually via devtools.

To exercise a right or ask a question, email
[privacy@gyandev.org](mailto:privacy@gyandev.org). We respond, where
practical, within 48 hours.

## Children's Privacy

GyanDev is not directed at children under 13 (COPPA and GDPR-K thresholds).
We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Parents or guardians with
concerns can contact [privacy@gyandev.org](mailto:privacy@gyandev.org) and we
will act promptly.

## International Data Transfers

The site is served globally via Cloudflare's edge network. Static content is
served from the edge nearest you. No identifiable personal data is
transferred cross-border by us.

## Security

- HTTPS is enforced on every route.
- Because no identifiable data is stored on our servers, there is no
  server-side data store to breach.
- Third parties (GitHub, Cloudflare) apply their own security practices to
  the services they provide.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves or the law changes. Material
updates will be announced via the GyanDev RSS feed and the project's GitHub
repository. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects
the most recent revision.

## Jurisdiction

GyanDev is operated from India. This policy is written to comply with:

- **DPDPA 2023** (Digital Personal Data Protection Act — India).
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation — EU / EEA).
- **UK-DPA 2018 / UK-GDPR** (United Kingdom).
- **CCPA / CPRA** (California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended).

Disputes concerning this policy are governed by Indian law unless a
mandatory rule of your local law applies.
